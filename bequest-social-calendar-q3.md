# Bequest Digital — 90-Day Organic Social Calendar (v2)
**Run:** Monday, July 20 – Friday, October 16, 2026 (13 weeks)
**Cadence:** Mon / Wed / Fri — all posts to FB + IG; X gets the same post trimmed or threaded
**Post types:** WHY (purpose/Golden Circle) · TIP (tactical) · POV (commentary/contrarian) · PROOF (credibility) · ASK (direct CTA)

**Audience:** Churches, Christian businesses, small-to-mid nonprofits, small business owners who'd rather build than market. Sensibility: mission-driven, stewardship-minded, skeptical of corporate marketing slop and Big Tech dependence. Values ownership, craftsmanship, permanence, and plain speech. Faith woven in naturally — never performative, never preachy.

**Framing spine:** Sinek's Golden Circle (Why → How → What) as the strategic frame, StoryBrand as the messaging frame. The two stack: your *why* is the story; your customer is the hero; clarity carries both. Mission-driven organizations have the strongest "why" in the room — most just bury it under a "what" website.

**Asset rule:** Every post must be fully producible by AI — text + generated graphics only (quote cards, tip cards, carousels, diagrams, stat graphics). No photos, video, screen recordings, or anything requiring a human behind a camera.

## Standing Notes

- **Voice:** Plain, direct, conviction without preachiness. Faith references natural and occasional — stewardship, calling, service, permanence — never forced into every post. Write like a craftsman, not a marketer. No hype vocabulary ("unlock," "supercharge," "game-changing").
- **Audience register:** Comfortable with ownership/anti-dependence framing (rented land, your list is yours, built to last), skeptical of corporate slop, respects competence and finished work. Never partisan, never culture-war bait — the sensibility shows in tone, not topics.
- **Golden Circle thread:** WHY posts bookend the quarter (Weeks 1 and 12); Why → How → What ordering appears in audits and frameworks throughout. Sinek and StoryBrand are cited by name where useful — familiar authorities for this audience.
- **Asset production (100% AI-generatable):** Five repeatable Poaster templates cover everything — quote card, tip/checklist card, carousel frame, diagram/wireframe, stat graphic. No photos, video, or screen captures anywhere in the plan.
- **X versions:** Hook + one supporting line; carousels become single strong lines or short threads. The ownership/craftsmanship posts (9/14, 8/19, 9/25) will travel furthest on X — prioritize those.
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 on IG (#churchmarketing #nonprofitmarketing #christianbusiness #smallbusinessmarketing base set), one or none on X, none on FB.
- **Engagement rule:** Reply to every comment within 24 hours.
- **Permission checks:** Weeks 10 and 12 PROOF posts use real client data/quotes — confirm with Tyler and clients before those slots lock.
- **Filler bank (swap-ins):** "Marketing terms in one sentence" series · platform-change reactions · one-post book summaries (Start With Why, Building a StoryBrand, Ogilvy) · "questions we get from churches" mini-series.
